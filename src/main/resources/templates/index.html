Presenting a single user number series game where your snake will be given 3 lives per game.
The number series is displayed in the form of eggs then you 'Crack' the math and find the next number in the series.
Then a set of random numbers will be displayed on board and you navigate your snake to eat the correct number that occurs next in the series.
If your snake eats the correct number, you and your pet will move to the next level where the complexity of the number series game increases.
Otherwise your snake will lose one life and will be staying in the same level.
